Near Expiry Logic
Items classified using shelf-life cutoffs per product specification. Filter: GOOD + ACTIVE only.
ⓘ Near Expiry Logic
Window 1 — 30 to 60 Days
Window 2 — 60 to 120 Days
Window 3 — Above 120 Days
ⓘ Near to Expiry — Calculation Logic
GOOD + ACTIVE only
How it works
Step 1 — Filter: Only records where Inventory Type = GOOD and Batch Status = Active
Step 2 — Shelf Life: shelf_life_days = Expiry − Manufacturing
Then: shelf_life_months = floor(days ÷ 30)
Step 3 — Cutoff Days (based on shelf life):
Shelf Life (months)Cutoff (days)
≤ 5 months30 days
≤ 6 months60 days
≤ 9 months60 days
≤ 12 months75 days
≤ 18 months90 days
≤ 24 months90 days
> 24 months150 days
Step 4 — Status:
If (Expiry − Today) ≤ Cutoff → NEAR_EXPIRY
Otherwise → OK
